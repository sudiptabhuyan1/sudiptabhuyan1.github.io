Biography

Ashish Verma has joined the Department of Electrical and Electronics Communication Engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur as a Ph.D. Research Scholar in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Debashis Sen. Before that, he received an M. Tech degree in Communication Systems from National Institute of Technology Patna in 2016 and a B. Tech degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from SSEC Bhilai(CSVTU) in 2013.

His research interests cover computer vision and deep learning, with special emphasis on eye movement data analysis. Specifically, he is dedicated to designing learning frameworks to model human visual scanpaths for dynamic analysis of still images. He is also interested in exploring learning algorithms for action recognition in still images and medical image analysis.
